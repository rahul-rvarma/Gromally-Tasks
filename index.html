Demand & Sales Forecasting Tool

Use this tool to determine **Unconstrained Demand** (how much to stock) or **Constrained Sales** (how much you will sell).

**Two Modes:**
  • **Leave Blank / High Value:** Result is **Unconstrained Demand** (tells you how much to stock).
  • **Enter Specific Value (e.g., 30):** Result is **Constrained Sales** (tells you how much you will sell).
